OUR STORY

Born out of a love of camping, Trailhead Journey hopes to bring you the camping experience we've always wanted. We've thought out and fine tuned the outdoor journey to combine two of our favorite things: camping and minimalism. It took us many a camping trips to be frustrated enough with how much stuff we had to have just to enjoy the outdoors, and when you get back home, where do you put it all? We're excited to bring you the outdoors in a much more accesible package.
